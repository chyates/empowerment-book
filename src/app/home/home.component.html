Crowd-Sourced Female Empowerment Book

What It Is

This is a collection of narratives and responses to questions about what female empowerment means to women of all ages.

Why We're Doing It

This will offer a multitude of perspectives on what it means to exist as a woman in society.

Layout of the Book

The first part of the book will discuss what type of gender inequality participants face. Then we'll cover the kind of tools participants used to overcome that inequality. Lastly, this will provide ideas for how to combat and prevent gender inequality in the future.

How to Contribute

Fill out our form and submit your answers. Select answers will be collected into the book but all will be collected on the website.
